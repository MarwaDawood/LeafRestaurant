Cancellation policy

We understand that plans can change. To ensure we can accommodate all our guests, please notify us at least 72 hours in advance if you need to cancel or modify your reservation.

Cancellations made with less than 72 hours of your reservation time a cancellation fee of £125 per person will be charged to the card provided. For any questions or urgent changes, please contact us directly at 020 7946 0123 or bookings@leafrestaurant.com . Thank you for your understanding.

Dress Code And Child Policy

To maintain the ambiance of our restaurant, we kindly ask that guests adhere to a smart casual dress code. Guests who do not meet the dress code may be refused entry, and a cancellation fee will apply. We appreciate your understanding and cooperation in ensuring a pleasant dining experience for everyone

Children aged 7 and above are welcome to dine with us and are required to dine from the same menu as adults. We are unable to accommodate babies and small children due to the nature of our dining environment, which may not be suitable for them. We appreciate your understanding as we strive to create a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all guests.
