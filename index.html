Welcome to Oro

At Oro, we believe that dining is an art form, and our passion lies in creating unforgettable culinary experiences for our guests. Nestled in the heart of London , Oro offers a sophisticated yet warm environment, perfect for an intimate dinner, a celebratory feast, or a special occasion. Whether you're savoring our signature seafood paella, indulging in a decadent chocolate torte, or simply enjoying a glass of wine from our carefully curated selection, every moment at Oro is designed to delight your senses.

Our Menus

Our chefs take inspiration from the rich traditions of Mediterranean cuisine, crafting each dish with the finest, locally sourced ingredients. From the vibrant flavors of Southern Italy to the delicate spices of the Middle East, our menu is a celebration of the diverse and timeless culinary heritage of the Mediterranean.

Private Dinning & Hire

With flexible seating arrangements that can be adapted to suit your needs, our semi-private dining area can cater for up to 20 guests. For larger groups, the entire first floor is available for hire as an event space or party room
